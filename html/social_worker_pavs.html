Deveshi is a dedicated social worker. She is associated with a NGO name People Aspiring for Vibrant Smiles (PAVS) since age 10.

In addition to this, PAVS has been conducting an Annual Blood Donation Camp every December.

She helped in blood donation camp, feeding hungry and orphans, distrubiting blankets to poor in winters, Taking Care of elder peoples's in Mother Teresa Home, helped renovating old railway station, she helped in raising funds by selling Diwali cards with paiting on it.
